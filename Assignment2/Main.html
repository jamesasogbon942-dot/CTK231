Major in Creative Technologies

The interdisciplinary Creative Technologies major emphasizes design and practice in the integration of digital technologies and the fine arts. Its curriculum includes sequences in Game Design, Audio and Music Production, and Interaction/Information Technologies. Students will study and practice topics including digital media, sound, video, electronic music, computer graphics, animation, interactivity, creative coding, immersive media, music composition, and emerging creative technologies.
