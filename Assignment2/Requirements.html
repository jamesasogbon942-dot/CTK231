Retention Requirements

  • Students in the Audio and Music Production and Interaction/Information Technologies sequences must maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.75 and a grade of C or better in all CTK courses.
  • Students in the Game Design sequence must maintain a cumulative GPA of 3.0 and a grade of C or better in all CTK courses.
  • Failure to meet retention requirements may result in removal from the program.

Standards for Progress in the Major

Students will be allowed to continue in the Creative Technologies program if they meet retention requirements and demonstrate satisfactory progress toward completion of the program.

Sequences in Creative Technologies

Accelerated Creative Technologies Sequence

High-achieving students in Creative Technologies (CTK) with a GPA of 3.5 or higher may apply to the accelerated sequence. This allows a student to earn 12 hours of graduate credit in CTK while completing their undergraduate degree.

Audio and Music Production Sequence

Provides study in sound recording, music production, and digital audio, including recording techniques, audio theory, mixing, and mastering.

Game Design Sequence

Emphasizes both the creative and technical aspects of game design, including gameplay mechanics, level design, storytelling, and immersive media.

Interaction/Information Technologies Sequence

Focuses on interactive and immersive media such as mobile apps, creative coding, VR, AR, and interactive installations.
